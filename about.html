About parhlobhai

A student-led platform for free, vintage-style resources.

parhlobhai is a student-led platform with the goal of providing resources for every student, regardless of their background. We cover O Levels, IGCSE, A Levels, Matriculation, Intermediate, and more.


Our resources are designed with the mental health of students in mind — study maps, cheatsheets, notes, study trackers, and more. All resources are free of cost, with no restrictions.
